Our Legacy

Since 1966, VMGN Fashions Private Limited has been the cornerstone of wholesale ethnic fashion in India, rooted in the ancient city of Varanasi — the spiritual capital renowned for its exquisite Banarasi textiles.

Now in its third generation, The GN Group represents a harmonious fusion of ancestral craftsmanship and contemporary manufacturing excellence. From the historic lanes of Golghar, we have evolved into a powerhouse serving the nation's largest retail chains.

Our e-commerce venture, golghar.org, bridges the timeless elegance of ethnic fashion with modern accessibility, bringing Banarasi heritage to discerning customers worldwide.

Every piece that leaves our facility carries the soul of Varanasi — the intricate handloom artistry, the richness of heritage textiles, and the promise of uncompromising quality that has defined us for over half a century.

Our Scale & Capabilities

VMGN Fashions Private Limited operates at a scale designed to serve India's largest retail enterprises. Our infrastructure, partnerships, and manufacturing capabilities position us as a preferred wholesale partner for bulk ethnic fashion requirements.

🏭

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing

Our Value Addition Center (VAC) at Rajatab, Varanasi is the first garment manufacturing unit of its kind in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, built in collaboration with the Government of India.

👥

Empowering Thousands

We employ thousands of skilled artisans, stitchers, and job workers from rural villages, preserving traditional craftsmanship while providing sustainable livelihoods.

🏅

Indian Handloom Brand

Registered with the prestigious Indian Handloom Brand, certifying our commitment to authentic, handcrafted textiles that represent India's rich weaving heritage.

🤝

LIVA Partnership

Proud partners with Aditya Birla Group for LIVA fabric sarees, combining premium materials with our craftsmanship expertise for superior product offerings.

🏛️

Government Collaboration

Our manufacturing excellence earned recognition from Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who met our Director Shri Mukund Ballabh Agrawal in February 2020 to appreciate our contribution to the textile sector.

📦

Bulk Order Specialists

Equipped to handle large-volume orders for national retail chains. Our streamlined production and logistics ensure timely delivery at scale without compromising quality.
